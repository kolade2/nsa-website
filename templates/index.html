Student run, Inclusive, and Community at BU

We are a Nigerian student club at Boston University dedicated to promoting cultural awareness and representation.Founded in 2020, we have been working to educate and engage the college community in understanding the rich culture and heritage of Nigeria.

Learn more

Who We Are

We believe in working together to create a sense of community and belonging for Nigerian students on campus. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive space for all students interested in learning more about Nigeria.

We welcome new members to join us in our mission and help create a positive impact on campus. If you share our values and passion, we would love to have you join our club.

We believe that representation matters and strive to provide a support system for Nigerian students on campus while also educating the larger community on the contributions and diversity of Nigeria

Upcoming Events

Join us for our annual cultural night on May 15th. There will be live music, traditional food, and a fashion show. All proceeds will benefit scholarships for Nigerian students in college.

Don't miss out on our panel discussion on June 1st. Listen to accomplished Nigerian professionals talk about their experiences and the importance of representation in their fields.
